Description

This handout provides an introduction to limits, a foundational concept in calculus that describes the behavior of functions as they approach a specific point.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the concept of limits and how they are calculated.
  • Apply limits to analyze the behavior of functions near critical points.
  • Solve problems involving limits and continuity.
  • Explore how limits can be used in real-world scenarios.

Key Concepts

  • Limits
  • Continuity
  • Asymptotic Behavior
  • Concepts of finite and infinite limits.
